Flow
Is an art interactive proyect.

The flow is the energy of plants wich traspases the air to get to the humans. The flowfield represents a tree.
It uses arduino to sense the proximity of a person and the energy will travel through the air to get to them.

In this stage, The mouse position represents the spectators proximity.
